You can now break down the **Top Pages**, **Entry Pages** and **Exit Pages** reports by full URL, instead of just path. See exactly where traffic is going across your subdomains like website, app, docs and more.

To enable it, open the **⋮** menu in the top-right corner of the report and select **URL**. We'll remember your choice for future visits.

[Learn more about tracking subdomains](https://plausible.io/docs/subdomain-hostname-filter).
