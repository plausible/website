Y﻿ou will now see a dedicated **AI Assistants** channel in your Channels report. It includes eight AI traffic sources (full list here).

We've also improved how sources are recognized and grouped throughout Plausible:

**AI assistants**

* Google Gemini is now reported as its own source instead of being grouped under Google Organic
* Different referral URLs from Perplexity and Microsoft Copilot are now consolidated into cleaner source entries

**Social platforms**

* Bluesky, Mastodon and Threads are now grouped under **Organic Social**
* Twitter is now reported as **X (Twitter)**
* Mastodon instances are consolidated under a single Mastodon source while still allowing you to drill down into individual instances
* Different referral URLs from Bluesky and Threads are now consolidated into cleaner source entries

**Other improvements**

* Kagi is now classified as **Organic Search**
* Different referral URLs from Microsoft 365 are now consolidated into a single source
