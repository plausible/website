You can now see the actual paths visitors take through your site with our new User Journeys feature.

Analyze how people move from page to page, discover the most common paths leading to conversions and uncover where visitors drop off before taking action.

User Journeys work across both pages and custom events so you can understand complete visitor flows, not just isolated metrics.

To get started, open any page or goal and click on “View user journeys”.
