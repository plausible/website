You can now see the actual paths visitors take through your site with the new User Journeys feature.

Analyze journeys from both a starting point and end point perspective to understand how visitors move between pages and events, discover the most common paths leading to conversions, uncover drop-off points and see conversion rates for each journey.

To get started, open the **Explore** tab from the report navigation bar alongside Goals, Properties and Funnels.
