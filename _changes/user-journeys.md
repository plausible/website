You can now see the actual paths visitors take through your site with the new User Journeys feature.

Analyze how visitors move between pages and events, discover the most common paths leading to conversions, uncover drop-off points and see conversion rates between each step.

Explore journeys from both a starting point and end point perspective to better understand what leads visitors toward a goal or where they go next.

To get started, open the **Explore** tab from the report navigation bar alongside Goals, Properties and Funnels.
