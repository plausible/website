You will now see a dedicated **AI Assistants** channel in your Channels report. It includes traffic from ChatGPT, Claude, Google Gemini, Perplexity, Microsoft Copilot, DeepSeek, Phind and Grok.

We've also improved how sources are recognized and grouped throughout Plausible. Here are some notable improvements:

**Better classification**

* Bluesky, Mastodon and Threads are now classified as **Organic Social**
* Kagi is now classified as **Organic Search**
* Google Gemini is now reported as its own source instead of being grouped under Google Organic

**Better grouping**

* Mastodon instances such as Fosstodon are now grouped under a single Mastodon source
* Different referral URLs from Bluesky, Threads, Microsoft Copilot, Microsoft 365 and Perplexity are now consolidated into cleaner source entries
